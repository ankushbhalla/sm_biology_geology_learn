ESPAÑOL EN ESTADOS UNIDOS
 

Un paisaje lingüístico bilingüe

 Texto en español 

Multiculturalism is one of the most distinctive characteristics of the United States. We can see examples of this co-existence of cultures by observing the linguistic landscape –that is, the presence of language in public spaces. Hispanic communities in particular become visible in cities in the United States through the use of Spanish. Here are some examples.
